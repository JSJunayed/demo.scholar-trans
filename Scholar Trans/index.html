HOW IT WORKS

Using the app is as easy as following these simple step

  • Firstly, the school transport must be registered with the school and undergo the approval process, e.g. verify driver's license, and provide all necessary information about the driver.

  • Once approved, they are added to the portal where they can be assigned to parents.
  • On the other hand, parents will install the ScholarTrans Mobile App from Apple's App Store or Google's Play Store on the phone to track the driver based in real time on the phone.

KNOW ABOUT US

We are committed to creating high-level solution

At ScholarTrans we are committed to creating high-level solution for every business around the world! Our solution provide system for managing the daily school transport operation that links the school, the parents and the transport providers.

ScholarTrans will offer real-time notifications, preferred pick up points and drop off points, and ability to call the driver from the app. The system provides an easy solution to everyday challenges with tomorrow's technology and innovation.
